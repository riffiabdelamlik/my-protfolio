02 — Impact & Results

How I work, in four moves.

Every engagement follows the STAR framework. Situation. Task. Action. Result. No hand-waving, no hidden math.

S

Situation

  • Mid-market B2B SaaS, ARR plateau at $14M.
  • Organic channel responsible for <6% of pipeline.
  • Content team producing volume without intent mapping.
  • Paid CAC climbing 22% YoY across LinkedIn and Google.
T

Task

  • Lift organic to 25%+ of new pipeline within 4 quarters.
  • Build a defensible topic moat in the integrations category.
  • Reduce blended CAC by 30% without cutting paid spend abruptly.
  • Stand up reporting the CFO would actually trust.
A

Action

  • Audited 1,200 URLs; pruned 38% of low-intent pages.
  • Built a 90-cluster keyword map weighted by SERP economics.
  • Launched programmatic comparison hub (412 pages in 11 weeks).
  • Wired GA4 → Snowflake → Looker for attribution clarity.
R

Result

  • +214% organic sessions in 12 months.
  • 3.4x SQLs sourced from content per quarter.
  • −38% blended CAC; paid spend held flat.
  • $6.2M attributable pipeline added in year one.
