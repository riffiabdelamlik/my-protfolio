Our Roots

Not just a meal,
an event.

We believe in the power of the communal table. In our kitchen, there are no shortcuts. Meat is roasted over open charcoal. Spices are toasted and ground daily.

Mansaf House is the culmination of generations of grandmothers' secrets, executed with the precision of a modern culinary atelier.

The Philosophy

Raw Materials.
Real Fire.

Charcoal

No gas. No electric grills. Everything touches local hardwood charcoal for a deep, primal smokiness.

Spice

Sourced directly from the souks of Amman. Whole seeds toasted and ground daily.

Time

Our jameed takes days to prepare. Our lamb is slow-roasted overnight. We respect the clock.
